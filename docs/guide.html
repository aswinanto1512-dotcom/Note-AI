Open the App ↗

How to Open & Use the App

Follow these steps in order — it only takes a minute.

1
Open your browser Use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge for the best experience. Both are free to download.
2
Go to the app In the address bar at the top of your browser, type or paste:
https://note-ai-ten.vercel.app  then press Enter.
3
Create your first note Click the big "+" button on the screen. A form will open — type a title, write your note, pick a category, and hit Save. That's it!
4
Enable notifications 🔔 Click the bell icon (🔔) at the top of the app, then click Allow when your browser asks. This lets the app send you reminder alerts.
5
Add your Gemini API key Optional To use the Ask AI feature, you need a free Gemini API key. Go to Settings inside the app and paste your key there. The app will remember it.

What Can You Do in This App

Eight powerful features — all in one place, easy to use.

📝
Create Notes
Write any note with a title, content, category, and date. Keep everything neatly organized.
Example: "Shopping list — Groceries — March 2026"
🎤
Voice Notes
Speak out loud and watch your words appear as text in real time. No typing needed.
Example: Dictate a meeting summary hands-free
📎
Upload Documents
Attach a PDF, Word file, or photo — the app reads the text inside automatically using OCR.
Example: Upload a scanned receipt and get the text extracted
⏰
Reminders & Notifications
Set a date and time on any note. Your browser will pop up an alert so you never forget.
Example: "Take medicine — every day at 8:00 AM"
🔍
Search & Filter
Type any word to instantly find matching notes. Filter by category to narrow things down.
Example: Search "invoice" to find all billing notes
💰
Finance Tracker
Log your income and expenses, upload receipts, and view charts to see where your money goes.
Example: Track monthly spending with a visual chart
✨
Ask AI
Ask any question about your notes and Gemini AI will answer — like having a smart assistant.
Example: "Summarize all my work notes from this week"
☁️
Cloud Sync
Your notes save automatically and sync across all your devices. Nothing is ever lost.
Example: Write a note on your phone, read it on your laptop
